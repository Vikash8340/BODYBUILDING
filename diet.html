Vegetarian vs Non-Vegetarian Diet

Vegetarian Diet

A vegetarian diet is a diet that consists of only plant-based foods, such as fruits, vegetables, grains, and legumes. This diet excludes all types of meat, fish, and poultry.

Non-Vegetarian Diet

A non-vegetarian diet is a diet that includes meat, fish, and poultry, in addition to plant-based foods. This diet can provide a variety of nutrients, but it is important to consume these foods in moderation and choose lean sources of protein.
