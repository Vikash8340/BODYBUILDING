Dumbbell Seated Shrug

How to Do Dumbbell Seated Shrug


  1. Sit on a bench with dumbbells in both hands, palms facing your body, back straight.
  2. Elevate your shoulders and hold the contracted position at the apex of the motion.
  3. Slowly lower your shoulders back to starting position.

    4. BARBELL UPRIGHT ROWS

    How to Do Barbell upright rows


    1. Use a handle attachment set to the bottom of the machine.
    2. Take a double overhand roughly shoulder width grip.
    3. Pull your elbows straight up the ceiling. Aim to get the bar to chin level or slightly higher.

      4. PIKE PUSH-UPS

      How to Do Pike Push-Ups


      1. Use a bench or an object to elevate your feet.
      2. Lower your head towards the floor by bending your elbows
      3. Push through your hands and return to the starting pike position.
      4. Repeat
