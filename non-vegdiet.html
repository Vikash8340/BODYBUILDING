Non-Veg Indian Diet

Breakfast

  • 2 egg omelette with onions, tomatoes and green chillies
  • 2 slices of whole wheat toast
  • 1 cup of tea or coffee

Lunch

  • 1 serving of chicken curry
  • 1 cup of brown rice
  • 1 serving of mixed vegetable salad
  • 1 glass of buttermilk

Dinner

  • 1 serving of fish curry
  • 1 cup of quinoa
  • 1 serving of cucumber raita
  • 1 glass of fresh juice

Snack Calories
